Golf

In my opinion, golf is not only one of the hardest sports to learn, but also impossible to perfect. This is why golfing is one of my favorite hobbies and how I choose to spend the short summers in Oswego, NY.

This website is meant to give you some golf history on the About Page, some fun facts about golf on the Fun Facts section and some helpful golf resources in the Links section of the website.
