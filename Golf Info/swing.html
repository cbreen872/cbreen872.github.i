Golf Swing

There are many factors involved when driving the golf ball. When looking at just purely distance, however, the driver is the best club to optimize the distance of the golf ball. The interesting thing about this is it does not adhere to the laws of physics.

According to the laws of physices he angle of the ball that should drive the ball the farthest is 45 degrees.

The loft of a driver is typically only 9.5 to 10.5 degrees.
