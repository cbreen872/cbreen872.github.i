Fun Facts

Here are a few fun facts about the game of golf:

  1. A common misconception is that the word GOLF is an acronym for Gentlemen Only Ladies Forbidden.

  2. Golf was played at the Olympics in Rio de Janeiro for the first time in 112 years.

  3. Golf is documented as being played on Musselburgh Links, East Lothian, Scotland as early as 2 March 1672, which is certified as the oldest golf course in the world by Guinness World Records.

  4. The word "caddy" comes from the French word for student, "cadet", which is pronounced cad-DAY

  5. Phil Mickelson, who plays left-handed, is actually right handed. He learned to play golf by mirroring his father's golf swing, and he has used left handed golf clubs ever since.

  6. English-American pro golfer Michael Hoke Austin is credited with making the longest drive of 515 yards, while playing in the U.S. National Seniors Tournament in 1974.
